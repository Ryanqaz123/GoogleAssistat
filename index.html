Welcome to Nimble!

What are the features of Nimble?
Nimble is a site that allows you to input a Google Meet code or any link and select a time. Nimble will automatically open the site at the specified time and notify you with a gentle ring.

How do I use Nimble?
Nimble is designed to be simple to learn and easy to use. All you have to do it enter the Google meet code or a link a site, give it a name, and enter a time you want the website to be opened. Then, press the plus button, and it should be added to the list of meets.

What if something is broken or I find bugs in the software?
Please email us at nimblescheduler@gmail.com, and we will look into it immediatly!
