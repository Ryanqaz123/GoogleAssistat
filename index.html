FAQ

What are its features?
Nimble allows you to schedule Google Meets or other links to be opened. When it is time, you will be notified with a gentle ring.

How do I use it?
Enter a Google Meet code or a link to a website, give it a name, and enter a time you want the page to be opened. Then, press the plus button, and it will be added to your schedule. The entries are automatically saved, so you do not have to input them again.

How do I enable pop-ups?
Press the button with the up-arrow icon. The website will try to open new tabs, and, if it fails, you will be notified, and your web browser will prompt you to enable pop-ups.
